What it is

An innovation for menopausal skin.

thanks to the anti-pollution complex. This protects the skin’s natural barrier with vegetal squalene, and instantly smooths the skin thanks to both hyaluronic acid and line filling pearls.

The production of the hormone, oestrogen, is significantly impacted by the menopause which leads to a slower production process within vital skin cells, such as fibroblasts.

Fibroblasts are responsible for producing collagen, elastin, and the dermal matrix - the skin’s natural bounce. It immediately leaves the skin brighter-looking and smooth. Day after day, the face is replenished, lifted, radiant, and glowing.


  • Skin type: Combination, Dry, Normal, Oilyy
  • Texture:Cream
  • Use: Apply to clean face and neck each morning, warm in your hands and press out over the face, over the top of your serum for best results.
